our story

Eyespike was founded in 2002 as a creative services company specializing in web development, corporate identity, and print design. Over the years, we have provided branding, marketing, and design solutions for companies across various industries, including manufacturing, fabrication, banking, professional services and the food industry.


Today, we have shifted our focus from client work to building and developing our own brands, leveraging our expertise in design, marketing, and business strategy. Our growth is rooted in a commitment to honesty, hard work, and trust, allowing us to cultivate strong, lasting brands while maintaining the values that have defined us from the start.
