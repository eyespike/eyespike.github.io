our story

Founded in 2002 as a creative services company offering web development, corporate identity and print design. Eyespike has since grown to combine its design expertise with marketing and business strategies to help brands achieve their business objectives. We continue to build our brands and customer loyalty through a slow and steady nurturing of our core values that include honesty, hard work, and trust.

What We Do

Define Strategies

To create a plan of action that will limit risks and provide the best chance for a positive outcome, every project needs careful consideration through research and experience. From the the seemingly mundane details, like choosing a domain name, to coordinating a year long nationwide marketing campaign, we pay close attention to every detail.

Build Brands

A well defined brand should effectively communicate who you are and what you do in a memorable way. Your Brand is more than just a name or logo, it establishes a customer’s expectations, defines the perceived value of a your products or services, and even who they are most likely to appeal.

Create Experiences

Every interaction with your company helps define an experience and an opportunity to build up or tear down your defined strategies or brand. We nurture every aspect of our customer’s experience and see the results of that through customer loyalty.
